ABOUT SBG

We, the undergraduate students of Gallaudet University, in order to form a more accountable, effective, and
representative student government that shall act on behalf of students in matters involving their interests,
including the promotion of high academic and campus life standards and facilitating of mutually beneficial relationships
with the administration and faculty, do hereby establish the Student Body Government of Gallaudet University.

Founded on January 1st, 1948
